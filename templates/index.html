About us

UDP Express offers you a wide range of high-quality express delivery services. Since the company was founded in 1998, it has grown and become one of the leaders in the transportation and forwarding services market, providing its customers with the highest possible delivery efficiency. We employ more than 2000 specialists in our branches around the world. We provide our clients with a wide range of services, including air transportation, Import/export transportation, Worldwide Transportation services, overnight delivery, customs clearance services.
